Proclamation of Love

On the last day of this year, I keep thinking about how I ended up choosing you, Shreya, without ever planning to. It started so simply a post about making friends, a message sent without expectation. We moved from Threads to Instagram, argued for hours, laughed at misunderstandings, even the fact that I once thought you were a guy. What began as debates slowly turned into something deeper. Somewhere between constant talks, stupid jokes, late nights, playful and intimate conversations, and even the sad moments, you became my constant without either of us saying it out loud.We didnt rush love. We grew into it. We were already loving each other long before we confessed it. And when we finally did, it felt less like a beginning and more like an acknowledgment of what already existed. Real, imperfect, honest. Just us. As this year ends, Im not making dramatic promises or pretending life will suddenly be flawless. Im promising something steadier. Presence. Effort. Choosing you again and again on calm days, on difficult days, on days filled with laughter and days that test us. Walking into the new year with you. Laughing with you, teasing you, holding you, growing with you.This isnt destiny dressed up in fancy words. Its me, at the end of this year, saying one clear truth: my heart feels at home with you, Shreya. I love you deeply, and I want to step into the new year and every year after holding that love together.

I Love You 💖
