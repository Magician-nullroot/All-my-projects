01
We Met on Threads
You made a simple post about wanting to make friends. I replied without expectation, just curiosity. One message led to another, and soon Threads felt too small, so we moved to Instagram. Somewhere in that early chaos, I even mistook you for a guy, which still makes me laugh at my own blindness. Fate clearly didn't care about my assumptions. It had already decided.
02
Our Debates Sparked
Our first real conversation wasn't soft or polite. It was a debate that stretched for hours. Opinions clashed, logic collided, egos danced. And yet, instead of draining us, it pulled us deeper. I didn't want to win. I wanted to keep talking. That's when interest quietly replaced argument.
03
Our Friendship Began
Debates turned into daily conversations. Hours melted without permission. We talked about everything—random thoughts, serious dreams, meaningless jokes that somehow meant everything. Friendship formed naturally, without labels, without effort. You became part of my routine before I even noticed.
04
Our Love Sparked
Love didn't arrive with an announcement. It grew slowly, hidden inside constant talks, inside laughter, inside comfort. We didn't confess at first. We were already loving each other without saying it out loud. The feeling matured on its own, patient and undeniable.
05
Our Happiness and Sadness with Each Other
We shared joy that felt lighter because it was shared, and sadness that felt survivable because we faced it together. There were emotional moments, vulnerable moments, times when words failed but presence didn't. Through it all, the bond only tightened.
06
Our Confession
When we finally confessed, it wasn't a surprise. It was a truth catching up to words. We accepted each other fully, without hesitation. That moment didn't start our love; it confirmed it.
07
Our Lovely Talks and Beautiful Stories, Sometimes Erotic but Ours
Our conversations became a world of their own. Funny, warm, sometimes bold, sometimes deeply emotional. Intimate in ways that were never empty, always meaningful. Those moments weren't just desire; they were connection, trust, and comfort wrapped together.
08
I Want You to Be My Wife
Now we are a couple, but my heart already thinks beyond that word. I don't just love you—I choose you. I want a future where you are my wife, my partner, my constant. I want a life built on our debates, our laughter, our intimacy, our growth. I love you deeply, completely, and without doubt.
